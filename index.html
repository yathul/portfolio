About Me

Hi, I’m a graphic designer,and animator who loves blending visuals, motion, and interaction to tell compelling stories. Whether it’s designing a brand identity, animating a concept— I’m all about making ideas come alive.

I believe design is more than just how something looks — it’s how it feels, moves, and connects with people. When I’m not designing, you’ll find me sketching ideas, exploring colors, or tweaking pixels just for fun.
